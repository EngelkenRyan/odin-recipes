Flat Iron Steak

Skip the steakhouse

Skip the steakhouse and make restaurant-worthy steak at home. Trust us, you're going to want to bookmark this irresistible flat iron steak recipe — you'll come back to it again and again.

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 pound) flat iron steak
  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh chives
  • ¼ cup Cabernet Sauvignon (or other dry red wine)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dry mustard powder

Steps

  1. Place steak inside a large resealable bag. Stir together olive oil, garlic, parsley, rosemary, chives, red wine, salt, pepper, and mustard powder in a small bowl.
  3. Pour marinade over steak in the bag. Press out as much air as you can and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours.
  4. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over top.
  5. Cover and cook on Low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes.
  6. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Sear and cook the steak in the hot skillet for 3 to 4 minutes on each side for medium rare, or to your desired degree of doneness.
  7. Discard the marinade. Allow the steaks to rest for about 5 minutes before serving.
